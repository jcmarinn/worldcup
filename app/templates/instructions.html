General Rules and Scoring System

_______________________________________________________________________________________________

For Inital Round (Round of 32):
  • You have to predict results for all 48 Games of Initial Round
  • For each correct prediction of winning team (or Tie) you will get 1 points
  • Additionally, for each exact Goal results (of both teams) you will get 2 more Points
  • Finally for each correct prediction of Group final position you will get 3 more Points

    •  

    After initial stage & no matter whom you predicted would be in next stage, we will all get to predict results For Round 2 again

    For Round 2 (Round of 16):
  • You have to predict results for all 8 Games of Initial Round
  • For each correct prediction of winning team (or Tie) you will get 3 points
  • Additionally, for each exact Goal results (of both teams) you will get 2 more Points

    •  

    Again after Round of 16 & no matter whom you predicted would be in quarter-finals, we will all predict again

    For Round 3 (Quarter Finals):
  • You have to predict results for all 4 Games of Initial Round
  • For each correct prediction of winning team you will get 6 points
  • Additionally, for each exact Goal results (of both teams) you will get 2 more Points

    •  

    After Qurter Finals we will all predict again for Semi Finals

    For Round 4 (Semi-Finals):
  • You have to predict results for both games of Semi-Finals
  • For each correct prediction of winning team you will get 9 points
  • Additionally, for each exact Goal results (of both teams) you will get 2 more Points

    •  

    Finally we will all get to predict results for the Finals

    For Round 5 (Finals):
  • For correct prediction of winning team you will get 15 points

    •  

    Whomever has the most points wins: we will award 70% of pot to 1st Place, 20% to 2nd and 10% to 3rd

    If there are ties then pots will be added and evenly devided between all ties
